The **Locations** breakdown shows **countries**, **regions**, and **cities** inferred from coarse network signals (not precise GPS).

## Behavior

- Values depend on what Kobbe can infer for each visit.
- Sparse or unknown locations may roll up to broader buckets or appear as limited detail.

Kobbe is built for **privacy-oriented analytics**. For how visitor identity and location are handled in general, see [Privacy and cookieless tracking](/docs/privacy-and-cookieless-tracking).
