Kobbe lets you turn the main overview chart into a branded image you can copy or download. Use it for social posts, launch updates, investor snapshots, or anywhere you want a clean traffic chart without rebuilding the graphic by hand.

The image uses the **current dashboard time range**, the **KPI selected in the KPI strip**, and your site timezone.

## How to share a metric

1. Open the site in Kobbe.
2. Choose the time range and KPI you want to show.
3. On the main traffic chart, click the **share** icon in the chart header.
4. Pick a background color for the exported frame.
5. Click **Copy** to paste the image into another app, or **Download** to save a PNG file.

The preview shows a landscape card with the metric name, headline value, range label, chart, and `kobbe.io` branding.

## What is included

Each share image includes:

- The selected KPI name and formatted value for the active range.
- A range label such as **Last 30 days** or **Last 30 days vs previous period** when a comparison is enabled.
- The main traffic chart for that KPI and range.
- A solid background color around the card.

Share images reflect dashboard filters and the KPI you selected before opening the share dialog. They do not include dashboard notes, pinned chart tooltips, or breakdown tables.

## Background colors

Choose one of six background colors before exporting:

- Blue
- Green
- Gold
- Orange
- Off white
- Dark

The range indicator chip on the card matches the selected background, except on the off-white and dark themes, where it inverts so the chip stays readable.

## Tips

- Switch KPIs in the strip first if you want visits, views, bounce rate, session time, or revenue instead of visitors.
- Enable a comparison on the dashboard if you want the range label to mention the comparison window.
- Use **Copy** for quick posts in Slack, X, or LinkedIn. Use **Download** when you need to attach the PNG to email or design tools.
