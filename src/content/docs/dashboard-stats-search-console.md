When **Google Search Console** is connected for the site, the dashboard can show a **Search Console** card with organic performance next to your analytics.

## What you see

| Signal              | Typical use                                             |
| ------------------- | ------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Queries**         | Search terms that surfaced your site in Google results. |
| **Landing pages**   | URLs Google searchers landed on.                        |
| **Clicks**          | Clicks from search results in the reported window.      |
| **Impressions**     | How often listings appeared in results.                 |
| **Rank / position** | Average ranking hints where Google exposes them.        |

Data depends on **Google’s reporting**, your selected property, and sync timing—not on every Kobbe pageview.

## Setup and limits

Connection, property selection, and sync behavior are covered in [Search Console](/docs/search-console).
