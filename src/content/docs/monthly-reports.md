Monthly reports send a concise month-in-review email for a site. They are off by default and can be enabled from **Site settings → Reports and alerts**.

Reports use analytics data Kobbe has already collected. They do not add browser tracking, cookies, or visitor identifiers.

## What is included

Each monthly report includes:

- Visitors and pageviews for the previous calendar month.
- Top page.
- Top source.
- Revenue summary when revenue attribution has received paid events.

Kobbe sends one report per enabled site and deduplicates each month so the same report is not sent repeatedly.

## Who receives it

The report is sent to the email address saved in the site's report and alert settings. By default, this can be the workspace owner email.

## Availability

Monthly reports are intended for paid workspaces and are opt-in per site. You can disable them any time from site settings.
