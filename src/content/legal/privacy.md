Kobbe is built with privacy at its core. We collect only the minimum information necessary to operate the service. This policy explains what we collect, how it's used, and the choices you have.

## What Kobbe does not collect

- No cookies or localStorage identifiers
- No raw IP addresses (IPs are never stored)
- No personal profiles or cross-session tracking
- No fingerprinting

## Information we collect

When you visit a site that uses Kobbe, the following anonymous, aggregated data may be collected:

- Page URL (path only, no query parameters with personal data)
- Referrer domain (not the full URL)
- Browser and device type
- Country (derived from request metadata, not IP geolocation databases)
- Custom event names (if configured by the site owner)

## How we use your data

We use collected information to:

- Display aggregate traffic statistics on the site owner's dashboard
- Filter bot and spam traffic
- Improve the product

## Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell data. Analytics data is only visible to the site owner (and anyone they share a public dashboard link with). We may disclose information if required by law.

## Data storage

Analytics data is stored and processed by Kobbe to provide the dashboard, reports, and shared dashboard links. We keep analytics data only for as long as needed to provide the service and operate the product.

## Your rights

Because Kobbe does not collect personal data or use cookies, GDPR consent banners are not required. If you have questions about how a specific site uses Kobbe, contact the site owner directly.

## Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The most recent revision date will be noted at the top of this page.

## Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent to [privacy@kobbe.io](mailto:privacy@kobbe.io).
